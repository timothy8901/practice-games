How to Play

Create words from the letter tiles to score points!

📏 Rules

  • Words must be 4 or more letters
  • Every word must include the center letter
  • Letters can be reused within a word
  • Find 13 words to win

⭐ Scoring

  • Each valid word scores 1 point per letter
  • A pangram (uses all 7 letters) gets a 1.5× bonus!

🔄 The Twist

After each valid word, the outer letters you used get replaced with new random letters — keeping the puzzle fresh every turn!

⌨️ Keyboard

  • Type letters to build words
  • Enter to submit
  • Backspace to delete
  • Space to shuffle
