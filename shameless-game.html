    House Rules

    Tic-tac-toe, Southside style. You earn squares by knowing your Shameless trivia.

    • 1Players X and O take turns. Get three of your marks in a row, column, or diagonal to win the game.
    • 2Tap an empty square to attempt a Regular trivia question. Answer correctly to claim the square.
    • 3Feeling bold? Tap an opponent's square to attempt a Hard (Steal!) question and take it over if you're right.
    • 4A wrong answer ends your turn with nothing gained — the board stays put and play passes to your rival.
    • 5You can't re-pick a square you already own. Fill the whole board with no winner and it's a draw.
