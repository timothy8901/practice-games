HOW TO PLAY

Match the top discard by color, number, or symbol. Play all your cards first to win the round!

  • Skip 🚫 — next player loses their turn.
  • Reverse 🔄 — flips the play direction.
  • +2 — next player draws 2 and is skipped.
  • Wild 🎨 — choose any color.
  • +4 — choose a color; next player draws 4 and is skipped.

HOUSE RULES

  • Stacking +2 — pile +2s; the player who can't stack draws the total.
  • Draw Until Playable — keep drawing until you get a playable card.
  • Jump-In — play an identical card out of turn to cut in.
  • 0/7 Hand Swaps — 0 rotates all hands; 7 lets you swap with one player.
  • Swap Hands Wild — adds a wild that swaps hands with a chosen player.
  • Stay Hydrated — sometimes you must drink after drawing.
  • Slap (on 9) — last to slap draws 2.
  • Mad Libs (on 8) / Charades (on 7) — party prompts.
  • Silent Uno — honor-system silence; tap "Someone Talked!" to penalize.
