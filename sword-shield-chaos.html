Pokémon Sword & Shield: Absolute Chaos

▾ a fully-randomized GBA demake ▾

Controls: ↑↓←→ move  ·  X A  ·  Z B  ·  A/S L/R  ·  Enter Start  ·  Shift Select. Tap the ⚙ menu in the player to remap keys, save/load states, or go fullscreen. On phones, on-screen buttons appear automatically.

About this edition

Absolute Chaos is a randomized take on the Sword and Shield Ultimate Plus demake: wild encounters, evolutions, abilities, trainer teams, TM moves, level-up movesets and field items are all shuffled — while levels, HMs and key items stay intact so the adventure remains beatable.
