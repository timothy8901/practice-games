Notes for your Claude Agent:
* The X-Item Strategy: For an AI that might struggle with complex type-matchup algorithms on the fly, the X-Item setup strategy at the Elite Four is practically foolproof. By burning 2-3 turns using cheap store-bought items on the first Pokémon of each Elite Four member (while healing if HP gets low), Swampert's stats will max out, allowing the AI to just click a single attack command (like Earthquake) repeatedly to win.
* Navigation: I kept the directional logic strict and mentioned exactly what HMs are required in each segment so the agent won't get stuck without the right moves.


================================================================================
POKEMON EMERALD: OPTIMIZED AGENT WALKTHROUGH
Goal: Defeat the Elite Four and Champion as efficiently as possible.
Primary Strategy: "Mudkip Solo". Only use the starter Pokémon in combat.
================================================================================


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
0. GLOBAL DIRECTIVES (AGENT RULES)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- COMBAT: Use ONLY your starter (Mudkip). Never switch to other Pokémon unless the starter faints.
- CAPTURES: Catch 2-3 random wild Pokémon early on purely to learn HMs (Cut, Rock Smash, Fly, etc.). Do not level them. 
- ITEMS: Buy Repels and use them in caves/tall grass to avoid unnecessary wild encounters. Buy Full Restores, Max Repels, and X-Items later in the game.
- MOVESET MANAGEMENT (SWAMPERT): Final goal moveset is Surf, Earthquake, Ice Beam, Strength (or Brick Break).


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. INTRODUCTION & GYM 1 (RUSTBORO CITY)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Start game. Set clock in your room in LITTLEROOT TOWN (time does not matter).
2. Withdraw POTION from the PC in your room.
3. Go downstairs, talk to Mom.
4. Exit house, go next door (Rival's house), go upstairs, click the Pokéball, talk to Rival (May/Brendan).
5. Go North to ROUTE 101. Talk to Professor Birch.
6. CHOOSE MUDKIP. Defeat the wild Zigzagoon.
7. Go North through OLDALE TOWN to ROUTE 103. Defeat Rival (Spam Tackle/Water Gun).
8. Return South to LITTLEROOT. Talk to Birch to get the POKÉDEX. Talk to Mom to get RUNNING SHOES.
9. Go West to ROUTE 102. Catch 1 wild Zigzagoon (to use later for the HM Cut).
10. Arrive in PETALBURG CITY. Enter Gym, talk to Norman, assist Wally in catching Ralts.
11. Go West, then North through ROUTE 104 and PETALBURG WOODS. Defeat the Aqua Grunt.
12. Arrive in RUSTBORO CITY.
13. Enter GYM 1. Defeat Leader ROXANNE. (Marshtomp sweeps with Water Gun/Mud Slap). 
14. Receive STONE BADGE. 
15. Go West from the Gym, get HM01 (Cut) from the Cutter's House. Teach Cut to Zigzagoon.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. GYM 2 (DEWFORD TOWN) & GYM 3 (MAUVILLE CITY)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Go East to ROUTE 116. Enter RUSTURF TUNNEL.
2. Defeat Aqua Grunt, rescue Peeko, get DEVON GOODS.
3. Return to RUSTBORO. Talk to Devon employee, meet President Stone, receive POKÉNAV and LETTER.
4. Go South through Petalburg Woods to Mr. Briney's cottage on ROUTE 104. 
5. Sail to DEWFORD TOWN.
6. Enter GYM 2. Defeat Leader BRAWLY. (Use Mud Shot/Peck if evolved, or just overpower). 
7. Receive KNUCKLE BADGE.
8. Go North to GRANITE CAVE. Navigate to the bottom (ignore dark room visibility, just hug walls). Talk to STEVEN, give him the LETTER. Get TM47 (Steel Wing).
9. Return to Briney, sail to SLATEPORT CITY.
10. Go to SHIPYARD, talk to Dock. Go to OCEANIC MUSEUM.
11. Pay 50 entrance fee, go upstairs, defeat two Aqua Grunts. Give Devon Goods to Capt. Stern.
12. Go North to ROUTE 110. 
13. RIVAL FIGHT: Defeat Rival under Cycling Road (Crucial: Use Mud Shot on their Grovyle).
14. Arrive in MAUVILLE CITY.
15. Get HM06 (Rock Smash) from the house Southeast of the Gym. Teach it to Zigzagoon.
16. Go to the Bike Shop, get the MACH BIKE.
17. Defeat Wally in front of the Gym.
18. Enter GYM 3. Defeat Leader WATTSON. (Marshtomp is immune to Electric. Sweep with Mud Shot). 
19. Receive DYNAMO BADGE.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. GYM 4 (LAVARIDGE TOWN)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Go North to ROUTE 111. Use Rock Smash to clear the path.
2. Go West to ROUTE 112, through FIERY PATH, to ROUTE 113 (collect volcanic ash, optional).
3. Arrive in FALLARBOR TOWN. Go West to ROUTE 114, enter METEOR FALLS.
4. Witness Team Magma/Aqua event. 
5. Return South to ROUTE 112. Take the CABLE CAR up MT. CHIMNEY.
6. Defeat Magma Leader MAXIE. (Use Water/Ground moves).
7. Go South down JAGGED PASS to LAVARIDGE TOWN.
8. Enter GYM 4. Defeat Leader FLANNERY. (Marshtomp sweeps with Mud Shot/Water Gun).
9. Receive HEAT BADGE. 
10. Exit Gym. Rival gives you GO-GOGGLES.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. GYM 5 (PETALBURG CITY) & GYM 6 (FORTREE CITY)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Go East to the desert (Route 111), drop down ledges, or Fly/Walk back to PETALBURG CITY.
2. Enter GYM 5. Defeat Leader NORMAN (Your Dad). He uses Normal types. Overpower with Swampert's strongest attacks. Use items if Slaking hits too hard.
3. Receive BALANCE BADGE.
4. Visit Wally's Dad next door to get HM03 (Surf). Teach Surf to Swampert.
5. Return to MAUVILLE CITY. Go East to ROUTE 118. Surf across the water.
6. Talk to Steven on the beach.
7. Go North to ROUTE 119. Enter the WEATHER INSTITUTE. Defeat Team Aqua. Receive CASTFORM (can use as a backup/HM user).
8. Cross the bridge. Defeat Rival. Receive HM02 (Fly). Teach Fly to a caught bird (e.g., Zubat/Wingull/Tropius).
9. Arrive in FORTREE CITY. The Gym is blocked by an invisible object.
10. Go East to ROUTE 120. Meet Steven on the bridge. He gives you the DEVON SCOPE.
11. Return to FORTREE. Use Devon Scope to reveal the Kecleon blocking the Gym.
12. Enter GYM 6. Defeat Leader WINONA. (Use Surf and strong neutral attacks).
13. Receive FEATHER BADGE.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. GYM 7 (MOSSDEEP CITY)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Go East through ROUTE 120 and 121. Arrive at LILYCOVE CITY.
2. Heal. Go to the Department Store. BUY: 15 Max Repels, 10 X-Attacks, 10 X-Speeds, 10 X-Defends, Full Restores.
3. Go West to ROUTE 121, Surf South to MT. PYRE.
4. Navigate to the summit. Talk to the old couple. Receive MAGMA EMBLEM.
5. Fly to LAVARIDGE TOWN. Go up Jagged Pass. The Magma Emblem opens the MAGMA HIDEOUT.
6. Navigate hideout. Defeat Magma Leader MAXIE again.
7. Fly to SLATEPORT CITY. Talk to Capt. Stern at the Harbor. Team Aqua steals the submarine.
8. Fly to LILYCOVE CITY. Surf East into the AQUA HIDEOUT cave.
9. Navigate hideout. (Crucial: In the room with 4 Pokéballs, the top-left one is the MASTER BALL. Collect it).
10. Defeat Aqua Admin Matt. The submarine escapes.
11. Surf East from Lilycove via ROUTE 124 to MOSSDEEP CITY.
12. Enter GYM 7. Defeat Leaders TATE & LIZA (Double Battle).
    * Strategy: Put Castform/Zigzagoon in slot 2. Have Swampert spam SURF (hits both enemies).
13. Receive MIND BADGE. 
14. Visit Steven's house in Mossdeep to get HM08 (Dive). Teach Dive to Swampert.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
6. WAKING THE LEGENDARIES & GYM 8 (SOOTOPOLIS CITY)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Surf South of Mossdeep to ROUTE 128. Use Dive in the dark water patches.
2. Enter the SEAFLOOR CAVERN. Navigate the boulder puzzles (requires Strength and Rock Smash).
3. Defeat Aqua Leader ARCHIE. Kyogre is awakened. The weather breaks.
4. Surf Northwest to ROUTE 126. Dive and enter the underwater cave to reach SOOTOPOLIS CITY.
5. Surf to the West side. Talk to Steven and Wallace.
6. Enter the CAVE OF ORIGIN. Talk to Wallace at the bottom. Tell him Rayquaza is at "SKY PILLAR".
7. Fly to MOSSDEEP, Surf South then West to ROUTE 131. Enter SKY PILLAR.
8. Climb to the roof (no puzzle required on this first visit). Awaken RAYQUAZA.
9. Fly back to SOOTOPOLIS CITY. Watch the cutscene. Talk to Archie, Maxie, Wallace, and Steven.
10. Receive HM07 (Waterfall). Teach Waterfall to Swampert.
11. Enter GYM 8. Defeat Leader JUAN. (Use Earthquake on Kingdra, beware of Double Team).
12. Receive RAIN BADGE.


--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
7. VICTORY ROAD & THE ELITE FOUR
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. Fly to MOSSDEEP CITY. Surf South to Route 128, then East to EVER GRANDE CITY.
2. Use Waterfall to climb the cliff. Heal at the Pokémon Center.
3. Enter VICTORY ROAD. You will need Surf, Strength, Rock Smash, and Waterfall.
4. Near the exit, defeat Wally.
5. Exit North to the POKÉMON LEAGUE.


CRITICAL PREPARATION FOR AI:
- Ensure Swampert is Level 55 or higher.
- Moveset should be: Surf, Earthquake, Strength, Ice Beam (Buy TM13 at Game Corner, or just use Return/Brick Break if unavailable).
- Check Bag. Ensure you have ~20 Full Restores, 10 X-Speeds, 10 X-Attacks, and 10 X-Defends.


BATTLE 1: SIDNEY (Dark Types)
- Strategy: Use 1 X-Speed and 1-2 X-Attacks on turn 1 & 2 while facing his Mightyena. Heal if necessary. Sweep his entire team with Earthquake/Strength.


BATTLE 2: PHOEBE (Ghost Types)
- Strategy: Use 1 X-Speed and 2 X-Attacks on turn 1 & 2. Sweep with Earthquake. (Her Dusclops has Pressure, so monitor your PP. Use Ethers if needed between battles).


BATTLE 3: GLACIA (Ice Types)
- Strategy: Use 1 X-Speed and 2 X-Attacks against her first Glalie. Sweep her team with Strength or Earthquake. (Do not use Ice or Water moves as they resist them).


BATTLE 4: DRAKE (Dragon Types)
- Strategy: Use 1 X-Speed and 2 X-Attacks against his Shelgon. Sweep with Ice Beam if you have it, otherwise use Strength/Earthquake. 


CHAMPION: WALLACE (Water Types)
- Strategy: His lead is Wailord. Set up 1 X-Speed and 3 X-Attacks. Wailord mostly uses Water moves which Swampert resists. Once set up, sweep his entire team with Earthquake and Strength. (DO NOT use Surf). 
- If Ludicolo comes out, hit it with your strongest physical move (Strength).


END OF GAME. Register in the Hall of Fame.
